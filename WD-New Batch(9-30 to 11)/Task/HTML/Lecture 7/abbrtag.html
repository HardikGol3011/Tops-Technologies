History Of DRDO

DRDO was formed in 1958 from the amalgamation of the then already functioning TDEs of the Indian Army and the DTDP with the DSO.

Hoistory Of BARC

It was founded by Homi Jehangir Bhabha as the AEET in January 1954 as a multidisciplinary research program essential for India's nuclear program. It operates under the DAE, which is directly overseen by the Prime Minister of India.
